# Command-line Gone Fishing Game 🐟

<img src="https://fishingbooker.com/blog/media/hero-lures.jpg" width="500px" />

Created a CLI fishing simulator game that lets the player decide whether to keep or release randomly generated fish (consisting of a name, weight and value), review previous catches and release previously caught fish and chum the water (which makes fishing take less time for 3 rounds). The goal is to catch fish that result in the highest priced fish caught total at the end of the game. Players can only fish for 6 hours and catching a fish takes a random amount of time between 15 minutes and an hour and half. Players can also catch a valueless boot or a super valuable golden doubloon. Players can catch a maximum of 10lbs of fish.
